Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my profile information?

Navigate to the Profile section using the sidebar menu. You can edit your personal information, upload a new profile picture, and save your changes.

How do I change my password?

Go to the Security section where you can change your password, enable two-factor authentication, and manage your login sessions.

How do I manage my notifications?

Visit the Notifications section to customize which notifications you receive via email, SMS, or push notifications.

Where can I view my order history?

Check the My Orders section to see all your past purchases, track current orders, and manage returns or exchanges.
